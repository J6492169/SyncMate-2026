## 📂 Managing Your Folders

SyncMate allows you to choose exactly which folders move between devices. 

1. Go to the Sync Settings tab in the menu.
2. Click the Choose Folders button.
3. Browse your computer to select the folders you want to sync. 
4. Select the target location on your other device.
5. Click Save. 

The software now compares the files in both locations. It moves new or changed files so that both folders contain the same data. It does not delete files unless you enable the Delete option in the advanced settings.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data Security

SyncMate processes your data on your local network. Your files do not move through external cloud servers. The encryption protocols protect your information while it travels between devices. ## 💡 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you experience problems, use these steps to resolve them:

* App does not start: Restart your computer and try to open the icon again.
* Device not found: Move your devices closer to your Wi-Fi router. Ensure you disable any VPN software, as this often blocks device discovery.
* Transfer speed is slow: Large files take longer to sync. If you sync thousands of small files, the process takes more time while the app creates a secure connection for each file.
* Sync errors: Check the Logs tab inside the application. This window shows specific error codes if a file remains locked or inaccessible.

## 📖 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does SyncMate work while I am offline?**
No. The devices must share a network connection to talk to each other.

**Can I sync more than two devices at once?**
Yes. You can add as many devices as your network supports. 

**Will this slow down my internet browsing?**
SyncMate uses your local network, not your internet connection. File transfers happen inside your home or office network. You will see no impact on your web browsing speed.

**Does SyncMate delete files on my secondary device?**
No. It adds missing files. It does not remove existing data unless you specifically set it to mirror the source folder precisely.
